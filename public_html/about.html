About hackATL
 a little introduction.

Build A Business in 48 Hours

Emory Entrepreneurship and Venture Management (EEVM) is proud to host hackATL 2013.

Instead of a traditional hackathon, we decided to bring together a variety of skill-sets under the same roof. By combining business, technology and design, these projects will have the best chance of thriving beyond the weekend and become the next big startup.

Participants will build a business in three days and present their startup to VC’s and local entrepreneurs. If great ideas interest you, hackATL is right for you.


Why hackATL?

For three consecutive days, hackATL removes all barriers to innovation. By bringing together the brightest minds of Atlanta and providing them with all the resources needed, hackATL helps you take the plunge and creates enough momentum to sustain a company beyond the weekend.

Not a techie? Don't Worry.

Hackathons are not only for the techie. If you have any idea, big or small, that you want to turn into something real - hackATL is for you.

When and Where?

When: Friday November 22, 2013 at 5pm until Sunday November 24, 2013 at 3pm
Where: Emory University's Goizueta Business School (1300 Clifton Rd NE, Atlanta, GA 30322)
