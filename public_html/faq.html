Frequently Asked Questions
 confused? don't be.

Q: Who can participate?

A: HackATL is open to everyone who has ideas and is innovative to transform them into a reality. Basically, everyone is allowed to attend.

Q: How many people should be in a team?

A: Teams should consist of 4 to 8 people. You are allowed to build a team before the competition, but there will be a networking event the first evening for you to meet other participants to create or expand your team, or to join an existing team.

Q: How much does it cost?

A: It is FREE to attend HackATL. There will also be FREE food and drinks.

Q: Can we work all night at the venue?

A: Yes. Hackathons are marathon events, so the venue will be open for the duration of the event for those that wish to work through the night though not required.

Q: I am not a developer, can I attend?

A: Yes, we need people of all types to work on the ideas. In addition, we need designers, business analysts, advertisers, and a lot of raw passion.

Q: How can I participate?

A: You can join a team as a programmer, business developer, and marketer. Or you can choose not to participate in a team, and just attend our free workshops led by renowned industry professionals and professors.

Q: What should I bring?

A: Anything from laptops, power cables, extension cords, mobile phones, tablets to sharpies, sticky notes, and most of all, great ideas!

Q: Do I need to print out and bring a ticket to get in?

A: No. If you have registered we already have your info and we will have your badge ready when you arrive.

Q: Is there an age limit for attendees?

A: No. From high schoolers to senior citizens, we expect attendees of all ages and all levels to be able to contribute.

Q: When do I form a team?

A: You will be able to form teams at the start of the event.

Q: How do I get to the Goizueta Business School?

A: The building address is 1300 Clifton Road, Atlanta, GA 30322. For more detailed directions to the venue, check out https://secure.goizueta.emory.edu/aboutgoizueta/contact/maps_directions.html

Q: Where can I park?

Parking will be available for free on Friday starting at 4:30pm in the Fishburne lot. Parking on Saturday and Sunday is free. If you enter from the North Decatur side prior to 5pm mention that you are with HackATL to get free parking. If you enter from the Fishburne side it is free and the gate should be up. Fishburne Parking Deck 1672 North Decatur Road Atlanta, GA 30322

Q: Will meals be provided?

Meals will be provided for competitors, speakers, and judges (check the schedule to look at the list of provided meals). Workshop participants will be able to purchase lunch and snacks at Einstein Bros Bagels, located in the Goizueta Business School.

Q: How late can I stay at the venue?

The venue will be open the entire duration of the event. If you plan on staying overnight, please sign up on your account stating which nights you will be staying and remember to bring a sleeping bag and toiletries.

Q: Will there be mentors?

Yes, on Saturday you will have the opportunity to sign up for a time to work with mentors from various specializations.

Q: What is the open pitch session?

On Saturday evening your team will have the opportunity to pitch your idea to the general audience to receive feedback on how to further improve your product and business plan before official judging the following morning.

Q: What hotels are nearby?

There is a variety to choose from. Some hotels in the area offer discounted rates for visitors to Emory University, so be sure to ask for the Emory rate if available.
